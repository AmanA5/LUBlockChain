Q.1 What is your understanding of blockchain?
A.1 A blockchain is a time-stamped series of immutable records of data that is managed by a cluster of computers not owned by any single entity. 
    Each of these blocks of data (i.e. block) is secured and bound to each other using cryptographic chains.
   
Q.2 What is the core problem blockchain trying to solve?
A.2 Authenticity and security are the major core problem for the online records of transactions.

Q.3 What are the few features that blockchain will give you?
A.3 Key features of blockchain: Cannot be corrupted, Decentralized technology, enhanced security, distributed ledger, consensus and faster settlement.

Q.4 What all things does a block contains?
A.4 A block contains : BlockNumber, Trans Record, Previous Block key and Mining key.
